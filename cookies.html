How we use cookies

ParentPick uses minimal cookies. We only set Google Analytics cookies if you click "Accept" on our banner. We never set advertising cookies ourselves.

Third-party cookies

When you click an Amazon link, Amazon may set its own cookies to track qualifying purchases for our Associates account. We do not control these. See Amazon's Cookie Notice on amazon.co.uk.

Managing cookies

You can change your choice anytime by clearing your browser storage for parentpick.co.uk, then our banner will reappear. You can also block cookies in your browser settings, or opt out of Google Analytics with the Google opt-out.

If you click "Reject", we do not load Google Analytics at all.
