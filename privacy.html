Privacy Policy

Last updated: 16 April 2026

Who we are

ParentPick.co.uk ("ParentPick", "we") is a free information site for UK parents, operated from Belfast, Northern Ireland. Contact: hello@parentpick.co.uk.

What data we collect

We do not require you to create an account. We collect minimal data:

  • Google Analytics (only with consent): If you click "Accept" on our cookie banner, Google Analytics sets cookies (_ga, _gid) to understand which pages help parents. IP addresses are anonymised. We use GA4 property G-B76RM2NRLY.
  • Cookie preference: We store your Accept/Reject choice in your browser's localStorage ("pp_cookies") so we don't ask again.
  • Emails you send us: If you contact us, we keep your email to reply.

We do not collect names, addresses, or any data about your baby.

Lawful basis

Analytics: consent (UK GDPR Art 6(1)(a)). You can withdraw anytime via the cookie banner or browser settings. Site operation and replying to emails: legitimate interests (Art 6(1)(f)).

Third parties

Google: Processes analytics data under their terms. Google Privacy Policy.

Amazon: When you click an Amazon link, Amazon may set cookies to track qualifying purchases. We are an Amazon Associate. Amazon handles that data, not us. See Amazon's privacy notice on their site.

How long we keep data

Analytics data: 14 months in Google Analytics. Email enquiries: up to 12 months after resolution. Cookie preference: until you clear browser storage.

Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete data we hold about you, and to object to processing. To exercise rights, email hello@parentpick.co.uk. You can also complain to the ICO at ico.org.uk.

Children

ParentPick is for parents and carers. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
