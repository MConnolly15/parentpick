About Us

ParentPick is a free tool for UK parents

Who we are

ParentPick was created to make baby product decisions simpler. We focus on sleep products for babies aged 0 to 12 months, and we present clear options based on age and common sleep challenges.

We are an independent publisher. We are not owned by any retailer or brand.

Our approach

Simple. Answer two questions and see a recommendation that fits your situation.

Practical. We show why a product may help, what to check before buying, and a direct link to a UK retailer.

No signup. The tool is free to use and does not collect personal data.

How we are funded

ParentPick is free to use. We may earn a small commission if you purchase through links to Amazon UK. This does not affect the price you pay.

We do not accept payment for product placement. Recommendations are based on suitability for UK parents, not commission rates.

Important information

ParentPick provides product information only. We do not provide medical advice. For safe sleep guidance, please follow NHS recommendations and consult your health visitor or GP if you have concerns.
